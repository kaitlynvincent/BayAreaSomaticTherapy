Welcome to Bay Area Somatic Therapy Center

Many people find us because they have been recommended this modality due to stress, trauma, chronic pain, or unprocessed emotions. They hear that deepening their relationship with the body can help them live with more clarity, grounding, and fulfillment while developing sustaining patterns and habits.

At Bay Area Somatic Therapy Center, we support clients in just this. Our work grows from the belief that the body seeks coherence when given space, attunement, and compassionate attention. We honor your pace and sense of safety, sometimes beginning with talk therapy, education, or simple awareness before working directly with sensation. Whether you are seeking support with overwhelm, wanting to deepen your capacity for choice and presence, or simply feeling curious about somatic therapy, we welcome you. Your individual work not only supports your own nervous system, it helps expand access to community care.

Our integrative approach blends somatic psychology, mindfulness, liberation psychology, nervous system education, and relational attunement to support safety as the foundation of belonging, power as embodied agency, and connection as the bridge to shared humanity.

What We Provide

We support children, teens, and adults — professionals, caregivers, and those moving through stress, transition, or high-demand seasons of life.

  • Somatic CPTSD group therapy for those healing from chronic stress and attachment wounding
  • Sliding scale individual somatic therapy for personal growth and stress regulation
  • Support in finding additional care and referrals when specialized services are needed
  • Education in nervous system awareness, mindfulness, and regulation practices
  • Free 15 minute consultation to explore fit, questions, and next steps

About Somatic Therapy

Somatic therapy deepens our relationship with the body, helping us attune to sensation, emotion, and intuition before stress becomes overwhelming. It supports the mind‑body connection in order to foster clarity, peace, and a greater sense of direction in life. This work can expand our capacity to hold what life asks of us at work, at home, and in relationships. When we are regulated, connected to ourselves, and in our power, daily life becomes easier to navigate.

Locations Served

We serve clients in San Francisco, Marin County, and the greater Bay Area, with telehealth available throughout California. Sessions are offered online for ease of access, flexibility, and nervous system comfort.
