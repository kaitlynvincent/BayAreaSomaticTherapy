About Individual Somatic Therapy

Somatic therapy supports the mind-body connection by helping you tune into sensation, emotion, intuition, and the deeper patterns shaping your daily experience. Whether you're navigating stress, transition, burnout, or simply seeking more ease and presence, somatic therapy offers a grounded and compassionate path inward. Sessions move at the pace of your nervous system, sometimes beginning with talk therapy, parts work, or education, and gently inviting embodiment when you're ready.

Who This Work Is For

Our individual sessions support children, teens, and adults navigating stress, anxiety, depression, chronic pain or illness, trauma, relational challenges, professional demands, or a desire for deeper self-understanding. Many clients come seeking clarity, balance, and a stronger sense of direction.

What Sessions May Include

  • Talk therapy and pacing-based exploration
  • Nervous system education and tools for regulation
  • Somatic awareness, grounding, and resourcing practices
  • Expressive arts and imagery work
  • Somatic Parts Work
  • Somatic Experiencing-informed practices
  • Support for building sustainable habits and patterns

Fees & Accessibility

Individual somatic therapy is offered on a sliding scale starting at $150. A portion of all individual therapy fees directly supports our low-fee CPTSD therapy groups, helping expand access to community care.
