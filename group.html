CPTSD Therapy Groups

Our CPTSD Therapy Groups are designed for individuals whose nervous systems have been shaped by long-term stress, relational trauma, or chronic overwhelm. This closed 8-week group offers a supportive place to practice regulation, connection, and choice at a pace that honors your system.

Who This Group Is For

  • Adults navigating overwhelm, shutdown, or chronic stress
  • People who feel isolated or find relationships challenging
  • Those wanting to build nervous system capacity
  • Individuals who have tried talk therapy and feel something deeper is needed
  • Anyone seeking steadiness, connection, and embodied presence

What to Expect

  • A closed 8-week cohort (same members each week)
  • Weekly in-person meetings from 6:00–9:00 pm with a 30-minute dinner break
  • No more than 8 participants
  • Somatic practices, expressive arts, and group processing
  • Nervous system and CPTSD education

Group Structure & Cost

This group meets weekly for 8 weeks in person in downtown San Francisco near the Powell St. BART station.

  • Schedule: Weekly from 6–9 pm with a 30-minute dinner break
  • Group size: Maximum of 8 participants
  • Cost: $30 per session, totaling $240 for the full series
  • Payment: $240 due at the beginning of the cycle

Our Approach

Our group work is rooted in the belief that safety supports power, and power makes connection possible. Through pacing, attunement, and compassionate witnessing, participants slowly expand their capacity to be with themselves and others.
